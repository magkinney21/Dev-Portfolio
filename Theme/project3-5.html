PROJECT DETAILS



About This Project

Going Places is an app that lets users post travel tips about the places they've visited! All a user has to do to make a new post is sign up, and then the user will be routed to the home page to view a list of current cities. Users can add, edit, and delete posts on the city of their choosing and they can also see all of their posts on their profile page.

THE GOAL

As a group of three, we built a travel community, Going Places, for users to share tips (AKA posts) about their favorite locations around the world. The goal was to gain experience working on a development project in a group, while navigating a client's feature list. Team : Magdalena Kinney, David Hernquist, Josh Seipel

THE RESULT

This was a Ruby on Rails app.Full CRUD for two models, and custom HTML, CSS, and JavaScript (using the asset pipeline). We followed a Scrum/Agile process. Utilizing our users stories on Trello, we worked in sprints and would check in with our 'clients',(our instructors acting as clients) to get approval before deploying each sprint. Resulting in accurate wireframes and ERD's.

THE IDEA

The idea behind this project was to work in a group and have our instructors act as clients. The 'clients' provided basic wireframes and user stories. In some cases, these requirements were vague or incomplete. The purpose of this was to communicate with the 'clients' to have their feedback guide our sprints to return a product that mirrored their vision.

UPCOMING FEATURES

For our upcoming features for this app we are planning to add a carousel to the landing page along with a few more design tweaks. We will be looking a several gems to add some more user friendly features as well. Stay tuned!
