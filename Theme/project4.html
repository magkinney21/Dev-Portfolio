PROJECT DETAILS



About This Project

TumMe is an online forum for people with digestive diseases to post about their disease and seek support and advice to help ease the burden of having a digestive disease. Users can also search recipes that fit their diseases restrictions. There is a custom google search bar catered specifically for people with digestive issues, so that they are able to find out more information.

THE GOAL

Myself and one other member decided to build a Ruby on Rails app. We wanted an app that was similar to a forum or message board but without the style of one. Our goal through this app was to supply a place for members to support each other and share advice that may be helpful to one struggling with a digestive disease or issue.



THE RESULT

Built an app with Ruby on Rails that has 4 models. One representing someone using your application (a user), and three that represent the main functional ideas for our app. This app has validations for certain fields on our models, and complete RESTful routes with GET, POST, PUT, and DELETE. Uses SQL for our database. Use a third-party API With HTTParty to pull in recipes. Include wireframes that are designed during the planning process. Include User Stories Include ERDs Has semantically clean HTML and CSS.

THE IDEA

This project was very personal to me, and my boyfriend who created this app with me. We both suffer from life long digestive diseases, and have only found relief by becoming our own advocates and supporting each other. Doctors can only understand to an extent, but they don't live with it nor do they see the true reality of it. So this is a place were people with same or similar issues can support each other and get through this together!

UPCOMING FEATURES

For our upcoming features for this app will be to implement another third party api. We would like to add a food journal, a doctor locator, and a meal prep api. Create a landing page, and incorparate several gems To allow users to follow and favorite posts and recipes. Along with a gem for uploading user profile photos instead of using urls. We will create an admin and a mailer feature to send monthly newsletters for our users who submit their email. Stay tuned!
